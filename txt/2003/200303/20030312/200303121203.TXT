    Military Ranks军衔
    1.There have been various ranks in
    military services of the world.
    世界上的军衔是不同的。
    2.In Russia and Britain，they still have
    the ranks of Marshal.
    在俄罗斯和英国他们还设元帅军衔。
    3.In the United States，there has never
    been Marshal Rank at all，but they have
    General of the Arry，General of the Air
    Force and Fleet Admiral.
    美国从来没设过元帅军衔，但他们有陆军五星上将，
    空军五星上将，海军五星上将。
    4.Different Services use different
    words to show their ranks.
    不同的军种用不同的词标明他们的军衔。
    5.In China，General refers to General
    of the Army or the Air Force.
    在中国，上将指的是陆军或空军上将。
    6.Chinese 海军上将 is required to be
    translated into Addiral According to
    usual Practice.
    按照惯例中国的海军上将要翻译成Ad――iral这个英语单词。
    7.Colonel is called 上校 in all the
    countries.
    所有国家都把Colonel称为上校。
    8.But the naval Colonel Must use
    Captain to show the exact rank.
    但是，海军中的上校必须用Captain才能标明确切军衔。
    9.However，in all the armies and
    airforces Captain must be replaced by major.
    然而，在世界各国陆军和空军中，少校必须用Major
    取代Captain。
    10.The word of captain in the armies
    and the air forces is called 上尉
    in Chinese.
    各国的陆军和空军的Captain这个词用汉语说是上尉。
    11.As far as soldiers are cocorned.they
    have their ownranks，such as Private，
    Specialist Sergeant， master Sergeant.
    就士兵而言，他们也有自己的军衔，如列兵，专业军士，军士长等。
