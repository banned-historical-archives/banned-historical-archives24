    Standing Guard Patrolling
    站岗放哨
    1 Who is on duty today？
    今天谁值班？
    2 The first platoon leader.
    一排长。
    3 Do everything according to his instructions.
    一切照他的意见办。
    4 Now listen to me.
    现在听我讲。
    5 The second squad is standing guard.
    二班在站岗。
    6 Squad five is required to take its place at 22∶00.
    五班22点接岗。
    7 Squad nine is on sentry.
    九班在放哨。
    8 Squad one is ordered to go on withits job after
    midnight.
    一班午夜后接替九班巡逻放哨。
    9 I’ll go the rounds of guard posts at any time.
    我随时查岗查哨。
    10 Any thing else，Sir？
    首长，请指示。
    11 I OK it.
    我都同意。
    12 Obey orders in all your actions.
    一切行动听指挥。
    13 Let’s do our jobs.
    我们各司其职。
    14 Report to me if necessary.
    如有情况向我报告。
    15 I‘’m in the coopany headquarters.我在连部。
