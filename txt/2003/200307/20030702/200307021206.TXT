    Services and Arms
    军兵种知识（二）
    1、What are the main types of coobat planes？
    作战飞机主要有哪几种？
    2、They are fighters，bombers and
    reconnaissance planes.
    有歼击机、轰炸机、侦察机。
    3、Air coobat is the basic task of the fighters.
    空战是歼击机的基本任务。
    4、What is the main task of the bom-
    barddent aviation？
    轰炸航空兵的主要任务是什么呢？
    5 It can ruin the
    important objectives in the deep rear of the enemy.
    摧毁敌纵深的主要目标。
    6、What about the reconnaissance aviation？
    侦察航空兵呢？
    7、It uses several types of aircraft to
    gather inforration about the enemy.
    侦察航空兵利用几种不同型号的飞机获取敌人的情报。
    8、 Navy must be composed of
    surface ship unit and submarine unit.
    海军一定是由水面舰艇部队和潜艇部队组成。
    9、It also has naval air force，
    marine corps，naval base and so on.
    还有海军航空兵、海军陆战队、海军基地等。
    10、Navy carries out missions at the sea mainly.
    海军主要在海上执行任务。
    11、What are its main types of the
    combatant ships？
    有哪些主要作战舰艇？
    12、They are aircraft carriers，cruisers，
    destroyers，submarines，torpedo-boats，etc.
    有航空母舰、巡逻舰、驱逐舰、潜水艇、鱼雷艇等。
