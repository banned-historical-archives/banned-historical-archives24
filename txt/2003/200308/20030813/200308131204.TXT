    Net Warfare
    网络战
    ●本期主持人韩同来
    1.The Internet enables us to
    know many things of the world.
    因特网使我们能了解世界上许多事情。
    2.The net warfare by computer has no
    “front-line”.
    依托计算机在网上作战是没有“前线”的。
    3. Many military services rely on computers for information gathering，
    targeting，force deployment，etc.
    很多军事部门依靠电脑进行情报搜集、
    目标瞄准及军队部署等。
    4. Computers were not designed to be weapons.
    计算机原本不是设计为武器的。
    5.Adopting civilian inventions for military use as weapons has not been
    unusual.
    民用发明用于军事领域作为武器早已司空见惯。
    6.With a computer one can attack a
    website in country A from country B.
    利用一台计算机人们就可以从B国攻击A国的一个网站。
    7.Keyboard operators can also become
    trigger pullers.
    键盘操作员也能够成为抠扳机的战士。
    8.The future war will be a
    keyboard-to-keyboard one to some extent.
    未来的战争在某种程度上将是一场键对键的战争。
