    Counter-terroriss
    反对恐怖主义
    1・Counter-terrorism is a topical subject.
    反恐是一个热门话题。
    2.Every country should fight terrorists.
    每个国家都应打击恐怖分子。
    3.Recently，a jointanti-ter-
    rorism military manoeuvre
    of meeber countries of the Shanghai Co-operation
    Organization was held on the border
    of our country.
    最近，上海合作组织成员在我国边境举行了
    反恐联合军事演习。
    4.Do you know the greatest terrorist event？
    你知道最大的恐怖事件吗？
    5.It was the September 11th Attack in NewY ork.
    是纽约“9・11”袭击事件。
    6.Four planes were hijacked by
    terrorists almost at the same time.
    四架飞机几乎同时被恐怖分子劫持。
    7. Two of them respectively attacked
    the World Trade Center.
    其中两架飞机分别撞击了世贸中心大楼。
    8. Thousands of people
    died unnatural deaths.
    数千人死于非命。
    9. Meanwhile，another airliner
    crashed into the Pentagon.
    同时，另一架民航飞机坠入五角大楼。
    10. The Pentagon is another name
    for the Department of Defense.
    五角大楼是国防部的代名词。
    11. Terrorism has
    become a common hazard of the world.
    恐怖主义已成为世界公害。（本期主持人
    韩同来、李琳）
