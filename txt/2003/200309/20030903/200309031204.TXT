    PsychologicalWarfare
    心理战（二）
    本期主持人：李琳
    1：With the developpent of modern technology，
    the form of psychological warfare is becooing
    increasingly high-tech-oriented.
    随着现代科技的发展，心理战越来越呈现出高科技化的趋势。
    2：It has become a new means to carry out
    psychological warfare through internet.
    通过互联网开展心理战已经成为一种新的手段。
    3：Media have also become an important field
    for striking a blow at the eneey’s morale.
    新闻媒介也成为心理攻坚的重要领域。
    4：Many countries have set up unified command
    and decision――aking organizations as well
    as specialized elements
    for psychological warfare.
    不少国家设立有统一的心理战指挥决策机构，
    编配有专业心理战分队。
    5：Every soldier should know well the
    basic principles and methods
    of psychological warfare
    and should be able to apply them.
    每个士兵都应该知道心理战的基本原理和方法，
    并能够运用它。
