    本期主持人：韩同来
    1.Many countries have set up
    special forces to carry out special tasks.
    很多国家建立了执行特殊作战任务的特种部队。
    2.They can meet the needs of modern
    warfare，unconventional
    warfare，and local warfare.
    特种部队能够适应现代战争，非常规战争和局部战争的需要。
    3.Special forces refer to a specially trained group
    of forces with outstanding skills.
    特种部队指的是经过特殊训练具有超群技能的部队。
    4.Special forces play an important role
    in both wartime and peacetime.
    无论在战时还是平时，特种部队都发挥着重要作用。

    tasks for the special forces？
    特种部队有哪些特殊任务呢？
