    TheSpecialForcesoftheWorld 世界特种部队（二）
    本期主持人：韩同来
    1.The special forces are able to disturb the
    enemy‘’s battle plan.
    特种部队可以扰乱敌人的作战计划。
    2.They can also capture or destroy the enemy‘’s
    important objectives with sudden attacks.
    他们还可用突袭方式夺取或破坏敌人的重要目标。
    3.I know that the special forces
    rescue hostages and POWs，too.
    我知道特种部队也营救人质和战俘。
    4.Fighting terrorist activities also becomes one
    of the missions of the special forces.
    打击恐怖活动也成了特种部队的任务之一。
    5.What qualities and skills should the
    soldiers have for the special forces？
    特种部队的士兵应具备什么素质和技能呢？
    6.They must be extremely strong and have the
    best psychological qualities.
    士兵们必须有极健壮的体魄和最好的心理素质。
    7.They must be well-
    trained in individual tactics
    and combat skills.
    他们必须在单兵战术和作战技能上训练有素。
    8.Driving，swimming，climbing，parachuting and
    camouflage are the essential skills for them.
    驾驶、游泳、攀登、跳伞、伪装都是士兵必备的基本技能。
