    Long-Range Reconnaissance 远程侦察
    本期主持人：韩同来
    1、Gathering and safeguarding intelligence
    are vital to security.
    搜集情报与保护情报对于安全是至关重要的。
    2、The missions of the Military Intelligence
    Battalion in the U.S.
    Army take on renewed significance.
    美国陆军军事情报营的任务十分重要。
    3、Most of the unit‘’s soldiers
    are tactical counterintelligence agents.
    这支部队的大多数士兵都是战术反情报的特工。
    4、Company E is a critical component
    of the battalion.
    E连是该营非常重要的组成部分。
    5、It is called the airborne，
    long-range reconnaissance company.
    它又名空降远程侦察连。
    6、Its soldiers are dropp-ed into a designated
    area ahead of ground troops.
    该连士兵先于地面部队被空投到指定地域。
    7、It can infiltrate as much as 100
    kilometers into enemy territory at night.
    他们可以在夜间深入敌领土100千米。
    8、And conduct reconnaissance
    and surveillance while reeaining undetected.
    执行侦察和监视任务，而不被对方发现。
    9、The soldiers in the unit carry
    85-100 pounds rucksacks.
    告读者
    这支部队的士兵要背85磅至100磅重的背囊。
