    Long-Range Reconnaissance
    远程侦察（二）
    本期主持人：韩同来
    1、Military Intelligence Battalion‘’s gear includes satellite communication systees， high-frequency radio，long-range optical equippent.
    美军陆军军事情报营的装备包括卫星通信系统、高频电台、远距离光学仪器。
    2、They also have GPS units，video cameras and a basic load of ammunition，including grenades.
    他们还装备有全球定位系统，摄像机和基本的弹药量，其中包括手榴弹。
    3、A company is composed of 18 sixmember teams.
    一个连由18个小分队组成，每个小分队有6个人。
    4、They are“the eyes and ears” of the higher commanders.
    他们都是上级首长的“千里眼，顺风耳”。
    5、In Kosovo，they were inserted by helicopters in the borders of two countries.
    在科索沃，他们乘直升机插入到两个国家的边境地区。
    6、They sent 12 to 15 teams through Albania into Kosovo with the 1th Infantry Division.
    他们派了12个至15个小分队，和步兵第一师通过阿尔巴尼亚进入科索沃。
    7、Six-man teams trekked through the woods， respectively for 72 to 96 hours.
    6人一组的小分队，经过长途跋涉，穿过了森林，分别用72小时到96小时不等。
    8、If everything went according to plan，helicopters returned to extract the teaas.
    如果一切按计划进行，直升机回来把小分队接走。
    9、A mission begins with a warning order.
    一项任务以预先号令开始。
    10、It is followed about 12 hours later by an operations order.
    12小时后才相继下达战斗命令。
