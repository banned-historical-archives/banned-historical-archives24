    1.Traditional field hospitals
    have been out of date.
    传统的野战医院已经过时。
    2.But we still need a great number of doctors
    like Dr.Bethune.
    但是我们仍然需要大量白求恩式的医生。
    3.In the Malvinas War，Britain employed
    civilian ships for medical use.
    在马岛之战中，英国动用了民船作为战时医院船。
    4.The biggest medical ship was refitted with
    more than1，000 beds.
    最大的一艘医院船是改装的，能容纳1000多张床位。
    5.We must attach much importance to the
    first aid and evacuation in wartiie.
    我们必须重视战时救护和后送。
    6.We regard self-aid and mutual aid as
    aid measures and also as strategic tasks.
    我们不仅要把自救互救视为救护措施，还要当作战略性任务。
