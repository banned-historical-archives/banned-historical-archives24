    MilitaryCommunication
    军事通信
    1. Military communication is very important both inwartime and peacetime.
    军事通信无论是战时还是和平时期都十分重要。
    2.But many communication equipments and techniques are out of date.
    但是很多通信器材和技术已经过时。
    3.Tactically，we still need some transceivers and telephone apparatus.
    从战术上讲，我们仍需要电台电话。
    4.Generally speaking，we are moving into a new digital world.
    总的说来，我们正在进入一个数字化的新世界。
    5.Strategically，the armed forces of the developed countries are studying the digital battlefield.
    从战略上讲，发达国家正在研究数字化战场。
    6.Newly developed computer and digital communication technologies enable any one to communicate with others freely.
    新研制的计算机和数字化通信技术能够使人们自由通信联络。
