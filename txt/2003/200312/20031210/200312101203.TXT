    MilitarySurvey
    军事测绘（一）
    ――――本期主持人：韩同来 牛晓莉――――
    1.Map reading and map using are the basic skills of the soldiers.
    识图用图是士兵的基本技能。
    2.Without military maps you don‘’t know where you are sometimes.
    没有军用地图有时候不知自己身处何方。
    3.The U.S.military started Desert Shield with very poor maps of the region.
    美军开始“沙漠盾牌”行动时使用的当地地图很差。
    4.These maps were improved a lot with the help of civilian satellites.
    在民用卫星的帮助下这些地图有了很大改进。
    5.Later，they used computers to convert remote sensing data into color maps of the theater.
    后来他们用计算机把该战区的遥感数据换成彩色地图。
