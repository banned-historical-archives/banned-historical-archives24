    MilitarySystem
    军制常识（一）
    ――――-本期主持人：韩同来 牛晓莉
    1.Our armed forces is called the Chinese People‘’s Liberation Army.
    我们的武装力量叫中国人民解放军。
    2.The highest policymaking body of the PLA is the Central Military
    Commission of the P.R.C.
    解放军的最高决策机构是中华人民共和国中央军事委员会。
    3.Immediately under it are the PLA General Departments.
    下属解放军各总部。
    4.They are the Headquarters of the General Staff，
    the General Political Department，the General Logistics Department and the
    General Armaments Department.
    分别是总参谋部、总政治部、总后勤部、总装备部。
    5.The PLA is composed of different arms and services.
    解放军由各军兵种组成。
