    Military System
    军制常识（二）
    本期主持人：韩同来牛晓莉
    1.The PLA has lots of military academies.
    解放军有许多军事院校。
    2.The University of National Defense is one of them.
    国防大学是其中的一所。
    3.Our largest army unit is military are acommand.
    我们最大的部队单位是军区。
    4.Armed forces are divided into group army，corps，division，brigade，regiment，battalion，company and platoon.
    部队分成集团军、军、师、旅、团、营、连、排。
    5.As for the ranks，we have General，Lieutenant General，and Major General.
    就军衔而言，我们有上将、中将、少将。
    6.The middle ranks include Senior Colonel，Colonel，Lieutenant Colonel，and Major.
    校级军衔包括大校、上校、中校、少校。
    7.The junior ranks are divided into Junior Lieutenant，Lieutenant，and Captain.
    初级军衔分为少尉、中尉、上尉。
    8.Soldiers are classified as Private，Private First Class，and Non -commis sioned Officer.
    士兵分为列兵、上等兵、士官。
