    RequirementsforCommanders
    指挥员的基本素质（一）
    本期主持人：韩同来牛晓莉
    1.Serious students of the art of war
    have been studying the future wars.
    治学严谨的兵法专家们一直在研究未来战争。
    2. Commanders must
    be ready to go into battle.
    军事指挥员必须随时准备投入战斗。
    3.High ranking officers have
    their strategy and tactics.
    高级军官有他们的战略战术。
    4.Junior officers should know the
    fundamentals of training.
    初级军官应知道自己的训练原则。
    5.They vow to offer up
    their lives for our country.
    他们都宣誓为祖国做出牺牲。
    6.Commanders are required to lead troops
    and have leadership.
    指挥员必须会带兵，有领导指挥能力。
    7.Leaders are expected to have the will
    and ability to defeat any enemy.
    指挥员必须具有打败敌人的坚强意志和能力。
    8.In routine training，you learn to make
    difficult decisions
    in complex combat situations.
    在日常训练中，要学会在复杂多变的作战形势下作出困难的决定。
