    UrbanWarfare
    城市战
    本期主持人 韩同来牛晓莉
    1.Policy-makers must consider military operations on urban terrain strategically.
    决策者必须从战略的角度考虑在城市地形条件下如何展开军事行动。
    2.Joint urban operation is an alternative to direct urban assault.
    除了直接攻城外，还可考虑使用联合的城市作战方式。
    3.If time is not pressing，sieges or neutralization will be likely options of choice.
    如果时间不紧迫，也可以选择围城或火力压制。
    4.In urban combat areas，street fighting cannot be avoided.
    在城区作战，巷战是不可避免的。
    5.Tactically，urban combat is primarily a small unit infantry intensive operation.
    从战术上讲，城市作战主要是小股步兵的密集作战行动。
    6.Infantry will dominate in urban warfare.
    步兵在城市作战中将占主导地位。
    7.We should allow civilians to get off the urban battlefield.
    我们应该允许平民撤离城区战场。
    8.Attackers can restrict the flow of personnel，material and information.
    进攻者可以限制人员、物资、信息流动。
    9.Fortified positions，heavy weapons，communication facilities are the key targets.
    坚固阵地、重武器、信息设施是主要目标。
    10.Only infantry has the“precision”to conduct effective urban operations.
    只有步兵拥有进行有效城市作战的“精确性”。
    11.Soldiers can perform all urban tactical tasks with their small weapons.
    士兵用手中武器能够执行各种城市战术任务。
    12.Joint commanders should adopt the right operating patterns to cross from rural into urban terrain.
    联合指挥官要采取正确的作战样式从郊区进入市区。
    13.Training preparations should offer joint commanders as many optionsas possible.
    训练准备工作应该向联合指挥官提供尽可能多的选择余地。
