    Battlesimulation
    作战模拟（一）
    本期主持人韩同来牛晓莉
    1.Battle simulation or simulation battle are based on powerful computers.
    作战模拟或模拟作战以强大的计算机为基础。
    2.Simulation battles have been carried out in rooms.
    模拟作战是在室内进行的。
    3.So they are called battle labs.
    所以又叫做作战实验室。
    4.All the services and arms can conduct their own simulated training.
    各军兵种都可以进行各自的模拟训练。
    5.Database and information technology are vital to this kind of training.
    数据库和信息技术对这种训练至关重要。
    6.Before “DesertSword”，the Americans simulated the ground attack on Iraque.
    执行“沙漠军刀”计划以前，美军模拟了对伊拉克的地面进攻。
