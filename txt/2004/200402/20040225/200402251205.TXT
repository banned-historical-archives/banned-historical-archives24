    TacticsandPrinciples
    战术原则（一）
    本期主持人李公昭姜镔韩同来
    1.Soldiers should know basic military tactics and principles.
    军人必须了解基本的军事战术与原则。
    2.Soldiers should also know offensive，defensive，cooperation，etc.
    军人还应该了解诸如进攻、防守、协作等。
    3.The purpose of war is to preserve ourselves and destroy the enemy.
    战争的目的是保存自己和消灭敌人。
    4.We can destroy the enemy effectively through active offense.
    我们可以通过积极进攻有效地歼灭敌人。
    5.Offensive operation is the main type of battle.
    进攻作战是战斗的基本类型。
    6.Through offensive operation，we can wipe out enemy‘’s effective strength.
    通过进攻作战，我们可以消灭敌人的有生力量。
    7.We should create favorable conditions for winning thewar.
    我们应为夺取胜利创造有利条件。
    8.There are many tactics for offensive battles.
    进攻战有许多战术。
