    TechnicalReconnaissance
    技术侦察（一）
    Reconnaissance troops seem increasingly
    important in local warfare.
    侦察兵在局部战争中显得越来越重要。
    Monitors must be forward eyes and ears toleaders.
    技侦监听员必须成为首长的千里眼、顺风耳。
    Without reliable information，victory is only a dream.
    没有可靠的情报，胜利只能是一个梦想。
    Almost every main power has its own reconnaissance troops.
    几乎每个强国都有自己的侦察部队。
    The USA，UK and Russia have powerful
    reconnaissance abilities.
    美、英、俄都有强大的侦察能力。
    Intelligence is an integralpart of C4I.
    情报是C4I的重要组成部分。
    Spies are still being used in modern wars.
    现代战争仍使用间谍。
    Technical methods are becoming more and more vital.
    技术手段变得越来越重要。
    Many specially trained personnel monitor radio and communication.
    大批受过训练的人员监听无线电通信。
    Monitoring stations are scattered around the globe.
    监听站遍布全球。
