    Long-Range Reconnaissance
    远程侦察（一）
    1.Scouts can go behind enemy lines.
    侦察兵能够深入敌后。
    2.Scouts can gather enemy information with their skills
    and tactics.
    侦察兵能利用技术和战术获取敌情报。
    3.Scouts can also destroy enemy command center if mecessary.
    如有必要侦察兵也可捣毁敌指挥中心。
    4.Gathering and safeguarding intelligence are vital to security.
    搜集情报与保护情报对于安全至关重要。
    5.TheU.S.Army has military intelligence brigades and battalions.
    美国陆军编有军事情报旅和军事情报营。
    6.Soldiers of these units are tactical counterintelligence
    agents.
    这些部队的士兵都是战术反情报的特工。
    7.Company E is a critical component of theunit.
    E连是该部队的重要组成部分。
