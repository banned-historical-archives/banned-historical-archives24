    Long-Range Reconnaissance
    远程侦察（二）
    1.It is called the airborne，long -range reconnaissance company.
    该连叫做空降远程侦察连。
    2.Its soldiers are dropped into a
    designated area ahead of groundtroops.
    该连士兵先与地面部队被空投到指定地域。
    3.It can infiltrate into enemy territory at night.
    该连可以在夜间深入敌领土。
    4.It can also conduct reconnaissance and surveillance
    while remaining undetected.
    该连能执行侦察和监视任务，而不被对方发现。
    5.Special Forces have a variety of missions.
    特种部队执行多种任务。
