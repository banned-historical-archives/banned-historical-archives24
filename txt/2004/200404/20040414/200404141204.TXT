    MineSweeping
    扫雷（一）
    1、Mines are used primarily by military forces for
    defence purposes.
    部队使用地雷主要是出于防御目的。
    2、Anti-personnel land mines are small，
    inexpensive，and lethal.
    杀伤性地雷体积小、造价低、杀伤力大。
    3、Land mines can be easily laid，
    but hard to detect.
    埋雷容易探雷难。
    4、Underwater mines are more
    expensive and sophistcated.
    水雷造价高且结构复杂。
    5、Many people and organizations have urged
    to ban antipersonnel mines.
    很多人和组织要求禁止使用杀伤性地雷。
    6、Minesweeping is the process of probing
    and clearing mines.
    扫雷是探测和清除地雷的过程。
    7、It is time-consuming and not always effective.
    它耗时多却并不总是奏效。解外供稿
