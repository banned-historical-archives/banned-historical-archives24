    MineSweeping
    扫雷（二）
    1.New mines with no metal limit a metal
    detector’s effectiveness.
    不含金属的新式地雷限制了金属探测器的效用。
    2.Trained dogs and special instruments can
    detect explosives in mines.
    训练有素的军犬和特制仪器可探出地雷中的炸药。
    3.Minesweeping tanks are used to clear
    minefields.
    扫雷坦克用来清除雷场。
    4.New devices are being developed to detect mines.
    正在研发新仪器来探雷。
    5.The Navy use special ships called minesweepers.
    海军使用专门的船只，叫做扫雷舰。
    6.Minesweepers can detect and sweep underwater mines.
    扫雷舰可以探测和清除水雷。
    7.Ships may also use noisemakers to trigger influence mines.
    舰艇还可以使用噪音发生器来引爆感应雷。
