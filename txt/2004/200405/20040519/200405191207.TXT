    MilitaryRevolution
    军事革命（一）
    1.Military revolutions have occurred
    periodically for centuries.
    许多世纪以来军事革命一直在周期性地发生。
    2.Military revolutions are ofen stimulated
    by major surges in technology.
    军事革命经常是由技术方面的重大进步所引发。
    3.Between the world wars，mechanized
    armored forces came out.
    在两次世界大战期间，机械化装甲部队问世。
    4.Tank consigned horse cavalry to the
    pages of history.
    坦克使骑兵成为明日黄花。
    5.Aircraft carriers rise to primacy.
    航母成为海上霸主。
