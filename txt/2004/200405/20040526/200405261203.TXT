    MilitaryRevolution
    军事革命（二）
    1.Srtategic aerial bombardment was
    established as a new way of war.
    空中战略轰炸成为新的作战方式。
    2.Nuclear weapons threaten to displace
    conventional weaponry.
    核武器大有可能替代常规武器。
    3.Some of the formerly dominant weapons
    and forces were displaced.
    一些以前曾风光一时的武器和部队退出了舞台。
    4.Now world military confronts the challenge
    of information revolution.
    今天，世界军事面临着信息革命的挑战。
    5.We can locate，identify，and track many targets
    at the same time.
    我们可以同时确定、辨识和跟踪很多目标。
