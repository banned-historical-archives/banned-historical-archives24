    MilitaryRevolution
    军事革命（三）
    1.New conventional and electronic
    weapons are being developed.
    正在研发新的常规武器和电子武器。
    2.The new systems combine great
    lethality with great precision.
    这些新的（武器）系统威力无比且极为精确。
    3.Strategists are thinking
    about future ways of warfare.
    战略家们正在思考未来的作战方式。
    4.The implications for strategic strike
    operations are potentially profound.
    战略打击行动的寓意是深远的。
    5.We must be fully prepared
    for the military revolution.
    我们必须充分做好应对军事革命的准备。
