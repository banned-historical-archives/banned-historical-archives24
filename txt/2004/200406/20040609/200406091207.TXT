    PsychologicalOperation
    心理战（一）
    1.PSYOP refers to planned activities to
    convey selected information to enemy audiences.
    心理战把精心挑选过的信息传达给敌方听众。
    2.PSYOP tries to influence enemy’s
    emotions，motives and objective reasoning.
    心理战试图影响敌方的情绪、动机和客观推理能力。
    3.PSYOP will influence the behavior of individuals.
    心理战会对个人行为造成影响。
    4.PSYOP is an integral part of military operations.
    心理战是军事行动的重要组成部分。
    5.Military PSYOP is divided into strategic，
    operational，tactical and consolidated ones.
    军事心理战分为战略心理战、战役心理战、战术心理战与联合心理战。
