    Psychological Operation
    心理战（二）
    1.PSYOP is carried out byexploiting
    propaganda media.
    心理战可通过利用宣传媒体来进行。
    2.Propaganda media are categorized by
    methods of dissemination.
    宣传媒体以其传播方式来分类。
    3.Propoganda methods are face-to-face，
    audiovisual，audio，and visual.
    宣传方法有面对面、视听、听觉和视觉四种方式。
    4.Face-to-face communication is the
    most effective means.
    面对面交流是最有效方式。
    5.Audiovisual media refer to television，
    tape recordings，and sound motion pictures.
    视听媒体指的是电视、磁带录音和音像节目。
    6.Audio media refer to loudspeakers and radio.
    听觉媒体指的是喇叭和广播。
