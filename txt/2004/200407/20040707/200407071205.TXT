    Logistics Support
    后勤保障（二）
    1.The logistic arm must seamlessly deliver material as needed.
    后勤部队必须及时输送所需物资。
    2.A lot of ammunition is needed to support combat forces’mission.
    支援部队作战需要大量弹药。
    3.The logistic arm must deliver material to the combat forces in time.
    后勤部队必须及时将物资输送到作战部队。
    4.The timely logistic delivery is very important to the success of amission.
    及时的后勤输送对取得战斗胜利具有十分重要的意义。
