    LogisticsSupport
    后勤保障（三）
    1.The logistic arm must seamlessly deliver material as needed.
    后勤部队必须及时输送所需物资。
    2.A lot of ammunition is needed to support combat forces mission.
    支援部队作战需要大量弹药。
    3.The logistic arm must deliver material to the acombat forces in time.
    后勤部队必须及时物资输送到作战部队。
    4.The timely logistic delivery is very important to the success of a mission.
    及时的后勤输送对取得战斗胜利具有十分重要的意义。
    5.Logistics will allow the forces to transit from peacetime to war footing.
    后勤必须能够使部队由和平状态及时转入战争状态。
