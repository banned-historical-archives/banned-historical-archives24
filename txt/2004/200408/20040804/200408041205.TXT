    InternationalSecuritySituation
    国际安全形势（一）
    1.Peace and development remain the two
    major themes in today’sworld.
    和平与发展仍然是当今世界的两大主题。
    2.The international security situation continues
    to tend towards relaxation.
    国际安全形势继续朝着缓和的方向发展。
    3.Worldwide，the forces for peace prevail
    over the forces for war.
    在世界范围内，和平的力量压倒了战争的力量。
    4.The security situation in the Asia-Pacific
    region has been stabie.
    亚太地区的安全形势是稳定的。
    5.The tense situation on the Korean
    Peninsula is markedly easing off.
    朝鲜半岛的紧张局势明显缓和。
    6.The situation in the South China
    Sea remains generally stable.
    中国南海局势总体来说保持稳定。
    7.Hegemonism and power politics still exist.
    霸权主义和强权政治仍然存在。
    8.The UN authority and role in handling
    security affairs are being challenged.
    在处理安全事务方面，联合国的权威与作用受到挑战。
    9.Local wars and armed conflicts
    have increased again.
    地区战争与武装冲突再度增加。
    10.Conflicts are caused by ethnic，
    religious，or other factors.
    冲突由民族、宗教或其他因素引起。
    11.Some countries have continued
    to enlarge military blocs.
    一些国家继续扩大军事集团。
