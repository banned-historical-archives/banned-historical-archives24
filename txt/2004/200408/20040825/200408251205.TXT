    ArmyDayReception
    建军节招待会（一）
    1.August 1st is China‘’s Army Day.
    8月1日是中国的建军节。
    2.To night，ourDefense Minister is giving a reception.
    今晚我国的国防部长将举行招待会。
    3.Hope to meet you at the banquet.
    希望在宴会上见到您。
    4.I feel honored to attend the reception this evening.
    能参加今晚的招待会我深感荣幸。
    5.The honor is ours.
    我们才感到荣幸。
    6.We gather here today to commemorate the
    77th anniversary of the PLA.
    我们今天在这里聚会，纪念中国人民解放军建军77周年。
    7.I am happy to celebrate the festival
    with our international friends.
    我非常高兴能与我们的外国朋友一起共庆佳节。
    8.We are pleased to receive the Korean military delegation.
    我们很高兴能接待韩国军事代表团。
