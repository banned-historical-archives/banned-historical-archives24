    ArmsControl
    军备控制（二）
    1.We have also tried to prevent the spread
    of increasingly detructive weapons.
    我们还努力防止扩散越来越具杀伤力的武器。
    2.Both progress and setbacks occur in arms control.
    在军控方面既有进展也有挫折。
    3.The U.S.government said it would withdraw from the
    ABM treaty.
    美国政府宣布它将退出反导条约。
    4.The U.S.also declared it would use
    nuclear weapons under extreme circumstances.
    美国还宣布在极端的条件下它将使用核武器。
    5.Russia and the U.S.decided to deactivate most of their
    strategic nuclear arsenals.
    俄美两国决定解除大部分战备核武器。
    6.In ten years，strategic nuclear warheads
    would be reduced to 2，200 each.
    10年内，两国将把各自的战略核弹头的数目削减到2200枚。
    7.Observers noticed the new treaty
    had a number of escape clauses.
    观察家们注意到：新条约里包含了一些有关退出的条款。
