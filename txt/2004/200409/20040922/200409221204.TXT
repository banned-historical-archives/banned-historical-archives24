    BalanceofPower
    力量均衡（一）
    1.Astate’spower is most effectively checked
    by the power of other states.
    遏制一国力量最有效的办法莫过于其他几个国家齐心协力。
    2.“Balance of power”means the equal power
    capabilities of rival states or alliances.
    “力量均衡”这个术语的含义是对峙国家或联盟旗鼓相当。
    3.The U.S.and the USSR once maintained the
    balance of nuclear power.
    美苏两国曾经保持旗鼓相当的核力量。
    4.A malitary balance of power was thus sutained.
    因此军事平衡得以保持。
    5.Threatened states often form a counterbalancing coalition.
    受到威胁的国家经常结成能够抗衡的同盟。
    6.States counter threats by allying with other
    threatened states.
    国家通过与其他受到威胁的国家联合起来抗衡威胁。
