    BalanceofPower
    力量均衡（二）
    1.Rapid changes will provoke counterbalancing actions.
    迅速的变化将会刺激抗衡行动。
    2.The balancing process helps to
    stabilize the relations between states.
    均衡的作用有助于保持国家之间的关系稳定。
    3.Occasionally a single state plays a balancer role.
    偶尔一个国家扮演均衡者的角色。
    4.A state may shift its support to
    oppose the strongest state or alliances.
    国家可通过变换支持来对抗最强大的国家或联盟。
    5.In regional conflicts，balance of power continues to work.
    在地区性冲突中，力量均衡仍然起着作用。
