    Leadership and Management
    领导与管理（一）
    1.Commandership is a special kind of leadership.
    军事指挥是一种特殊的领导艺术。
    2.Leadership is not something that one is born with.
    领导艺术不是天生就具备的。
    3.A good leader should be strict yet caring.
    好的领导应该严格但又要关心他人。
    4.Commanders must have a vision of the future.
    指挥员应当高瞻远瞩。
    5.A leader should know how to maximize his subordinaters‘’
    potentials.
    领导应该懂得如何最大限度地发挥下属的潜能。
    6.Leaders should listen to soldiers‘’opinions.
    领导应该倾听士兵的意见。
    7.Commanders must know their men and needs.
    指挥员必须了解部队以及他们的需要。
    8.Group cohesion and teamwork are important.
    集体凝聚力和团结协作十分重要。
