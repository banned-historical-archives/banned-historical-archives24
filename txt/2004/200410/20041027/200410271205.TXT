    Leadership and Management
    领导与管理（二）
    1.We should build trust between leaders and their
    subordinates.
    领导与下属之间应当建立起信任。
    2.We have reduced the structure of staff and organization.
    我们已精简了人员和机构。
    3.A leader‘’s skills are refined in operational assignments.
    指挥员可以在执行作战任务中提高自己的技能。
    4.The higher commander‘’s intent should be the focus of
    action.
    上级指挥员的意图应是行动的主要依据。
    5.Orders must be brief and clear.
    下达命令必须简明扼要。
    6.Maintaining a disciplined unit is a difficult art.
    使部队纪律严明是一门不容易掌握的艺术。
    7.Strict discipline is vital to any army.
    对任何部队来说，严格的纪律都是至关重要的。
