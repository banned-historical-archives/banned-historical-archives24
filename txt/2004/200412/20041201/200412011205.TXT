    Strategies in the 21st Century
    21世纪的军事战略（一）
    1.Security environment in the 21st century is
    very different from before.
    21世纪的安全环境与以前已经大不相同。
    2.In 21st century，threat will be uncertain and sudden.
    在21世纪里，威胁将是不确定的和突发性的。
    3.Current and future enemies might strike in surprising ways.
    现在以及未来的敌人可能会以出人意料的方式发起攻击。
    4.Armed forces must prepare for future wars.
    武装力量要为未来战争做好准备。
    5.Future wars might be different from the present ones.
    未来战争可能与现在的大不相同。
    6.New threats call for a new approach to defense.
    新的威胁需要我们有新的防御办法来对付。
    7.The performance of conventional weapons must
    be further improved.
    常规武器的性能需要进一步改善。
    8.Ground forces will be lighter and more lethal than
    those today.
    地面部队将比今天更加轻便、更加具有战斗力。
