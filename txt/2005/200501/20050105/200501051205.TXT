    Military Legislative Work
    军法工作
    1、China attaches great importance to the building
    of a military legal system.
    中国非常重视军事法律体制的建设。
    2、The improvement of the work is vital to defense modernization.
    这方面工作的改善对于实现国防现代化非常重要。
    3、It is also a guarantee for regularizing the armed forces.
    它也是军队正规化的重要保证。
    4、The NPC has formulated many defense and army-building laws.
    全国人大制定了多部国防与军队建设法律。
    5、They include laws about defense，mili tary service and other aspects.
    包括与国防、兵役等相关的法律。
    6、Other laws are about air defense，military ranks，
    military facilities，etc.
    还有一些其他的法律是关于防空、军衔、军事设施等等。
    7、The State Council and the CMC have worked out some laws and
    regulations.
    国务院和中央军委还制订了一些法律规定。
