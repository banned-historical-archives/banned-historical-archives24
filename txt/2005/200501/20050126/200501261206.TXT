    Military Legislative Wcrk
    军法工作
    1.Some military laws and regulations concern
    logistics，discipline，drill，etc.
    一些军事法律与规定与后勤、纪律、训练等方面有关。
    2.Morer ules and regulations been drawn by relevant organizaions.
    有关部门制定了更多的条令条例。
    3.Military legislation must be suited to its
    national and military environment.
    军事立法必须与国家和军事环境相一致。
    4.We also study the international military
    related treaties and agreements.
    我们也研究国际军事条约与协议。
    5.We pay due attention to international elgal norms
    and practices.
    我们对国际法律规范与惯例给予应有的重视。
    6.We promote publicity and education of law in the armed forces.
    我们非常重视在军队里进行法律知识宣传与教育。
    7.Chinese armed forces have made efforts to
    popularize legal knowledge.
    中国军队已安排进行了普法教育。
