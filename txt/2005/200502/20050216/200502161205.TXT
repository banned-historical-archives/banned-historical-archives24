    Foreign Military Contacts
    对外军事交流
    1.Foreign military contacts are a part of China's
    overall diplomacy.
    对外军事交流是中国整体外交的重要组成部分。
    2.Military contacts serve the modernization of national defence.
    军事交流为国防现代化服务。
    3.China deals with its foreign military relations independently.
    中国独立自主处理军事外交关系。
    4.Chinese armed forces are active in military
    diplomatic activities.
    中国军队积极参加军事外交活动。
