    Foreign Military Contacts
    对外军事交流
    1.China tries to develop omni-directional miltrary diplomacy.
    中国积极发展全方向的军事外交。
    2.We have established relations with many armed forces.
    我们已经与许多国家的军队建立了关系。
    3.China has set up over 90 militray attache's offices abroad.
    中国在驻外使馆设立了90多个武官处。
    4.Sixty countries have set up their attache's offices China.
    60个国家在中国设有武官处。
    5.In the last 20 years，Chinese military delegations
    have visited over 80 countries.
    过去的20多年里，中国军事代表团访问了80多个国家。
    6.Over 2000 foreign military delegations have visited China.
    2000多个外国军事代表团访问了中国。
    7.China has developed military contacts with adjacent conutries.
    中国与邻国发展军事交流。
