    SoldierA： China has a long border to defend.
    战士甲：中国有漫长的国境线需要保卫。
    SoldierB： Right.The border guards have a tough job todo.
    战士乙：是啊！边防战士的工作很辛苦。
    SoldierA： Do they still patrol on horse back？
    战士甲：他们还骑马巡逻吗？
    SoldierB： Of course not！ It is the 21st century now！
    战士乙：当然不骑啦！现在已经是21世纪啦！
    SoldierA： What do they ride？
    战士甲：那他们乘什么（去巡逻）？
    SoldierB： They ride in patrol vehicles and helicopters.
    战士乙：乘巡逻车和直升机。
