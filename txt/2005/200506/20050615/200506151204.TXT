    Requirements for Commanders
    指挥员的基本素质
    Captain：What are the basic qualities of a good commander in you mind？
    上尉：你认为优秀的指挥官应该具备什么样的素质？
    Major：First，heshould be loyal to his country.
    少校：首先，他必须忠于祖国。
    Captain：And second？
    上尉：那么第二呢？
    Major：He must have an iron will to lead and win.
    少校：他必须具有钢铁般的意志去指挥部队和取得胜利。
    Captain：And third？
    上尉：第三呢？
    Major：He must be knowledgeable.
    少校：他必须知识渊博。
