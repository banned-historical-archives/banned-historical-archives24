    Ground Assaults
    地面进攻
    Captain： What do you think about ground assaults nowadays？
    上尉：你对当今的地面进攻怎么看？
    Major： Ground assaults cannot be separated from joint attacks.
    少校：地面进攻与合成作战是分不开的。
    Captain：To be more exact？
    上尉：您能不能说具体一点儿？
    Major：Air strikes usually come before a ground assault is launched.
    少校：在地面进攻开始之前通常要进行空中打击。
    Captain：I think that helps to reduce casualties on the foot soldiers.
    上尉：  我认为那样可以减少步兵的伤亡。
    Major：Right.Infantries now move fast and safe in helicopters and vehicles.
    少校：没错，现在的步兵乘直升机与车辆运动速度快且安全。
