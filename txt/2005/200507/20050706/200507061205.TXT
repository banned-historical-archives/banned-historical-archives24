    海战
    Captain：Sea fight has been the main task of the Navy.
    上尉：海战一直是海军的主要任务。
    Major：There were many sea battles known to all.
    少校：大家熟知的海战很多。
    Captain：There hasn’t been any major conflict on the sea since the
    Malvinas War.
    上尉：自从马岛战争以来，还没有发生过任何重大的海上冲突。
    Major：But we can draw a lesson from the study of sea fights in the past.
    少校：不过我们可以从研究过去的海战当中汲取教训。
    Captain：What can we learn from the Surprise Attack on Pearl Harbor？
    上尉：从奇袭珍珠港当中可以学到什么？
    Major：Stay alert and never neglect any threat.
    少校：保持警惕，永远不能大意。
    Captain：And from the Battle of Midway？
    上尉：那么从中途岛之战中能学到什么？
    Major：Use your wisdom and courage to fight a cunning enemy.
    少校：对付狡猾的敌人要斗智斗勇。
