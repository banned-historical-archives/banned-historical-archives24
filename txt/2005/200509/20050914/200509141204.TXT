    Humor in Barracks
    That’sNot It
    A general noticed one of his soldiers behaving oddly.
    The soldier would pick up any piece of paper he found，frown and say，“That’s not it”，and put it down again.This went on for some time，until the general arranged to have the soldier psychologically tested.The psychologist concluded that the soldier was deranged，and wroteout his discharge from the army.
    The soldier picked it up，smiled and said，“That’sit.”
    参考译文
    不是这个
    将军发现他的部队里有名士兵举止怪异。这位士兵总是随便拿起一张纸，皱皱眉，然后说“不是这个”，说完就把纸放下了。这种现象持续了好一阵子，于是将军安排该士兵接受心理测试。心理医生的结论是该士兵精神不正常，给他开了一张退伍令。
    该士兵拿起退伍令，笑了起来，说：“就是这个。”（秦学瑛、葛敏）
