    Humor in Barracks
    Contest
    A soldier had already lost eight bicycles，so he locked his ninth
    bicycle with seven locks and left a not esaying：
    wait and see how you can steal again！Later he was surprised
    to find his bicycle having an extra lock with a paper：Wait
    and see how you can ride it！
    参考译文
    较量
    一名士兵丢了八辆自行车后，第九辆上了七道锁，并留了张纸条，上面写着：看你如何偷！第二天，他发现车子多了一道锁，也有一张纸条，上面写着：看你如何骑！（张希库、陈云云）
