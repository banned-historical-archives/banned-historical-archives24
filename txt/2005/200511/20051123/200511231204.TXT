    Feel like Colonel
    Tom，a recruit，was a bit of a loner.He took to drinking heavily whenever he
    booked out for the weekend.One weekend，he returned so drunk that he was told to see the Commanding Officer immediately.“Look，Tom，why don’t you shape up？”said the CO，who was something of a softie.“There is a real future for you here if you sober up.You could become a corporal，or even a sergeant！Isn’t that something to look forward to？”Tom replied，“Well，Sir，to tell you the truth， that’s really not good enough for me because after a few glasses of Tiger，I feel like a colonel！”

    感觉像上校
    汤姆是名新兵，性格有点孤僻。只要周末请假外出，他一定会喝个烂醉如泥。一个周末，他又酩酊大醉地回来，立刻被叫去见上级军官。“汤姆，你为什么不振作起来呢？”心肠软的军官说。“你清醒起来的话，在这里很有前途。你可以成为下士，甚至当上军士！难道那不值得你去好好努力一下？”汤姆回答道：“这个嘛，长官，跟您说实话吧，对我来说那算不了什么；因为我几杯‘虎牌’啤酒下肚，就感觉自己像个上校！”（陈海强、曹建勇）
