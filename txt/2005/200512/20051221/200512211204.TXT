    Bragging
    A career military man，who had retired as a corporal，was telling theyounger men how he handled officers during his years of service.
    “It didn’t matter a hoot if he was a Major General，an Admiral，or the Commander-in-Chief.I always told those guys exactly where to get off.”
    “Wow，you must have been something.”the admiring young soldiers remarked.“What was your job in the service？”
    “Elevator operator in the Pentagon.”

    吹牛
    一名职业军人退休时只不过是个下士，他对年轻的士兵们讲他当年服役期间是怎样对付军官们的。
    “我才不管他是少将也好，海军上将也罢，或者是什么总司令，我总是叫他们到哪下他们就得下。”
    “哇，那您可是个大人物啊。”年轻的士兵们惊叹不已地说。“您在部队里的时候做什么工作？”
    “五角大楼的电梯员。” （陈海强、霍锐）
